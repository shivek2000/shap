Our Services

Shap Auto & Electrical, we offer a comprehensive range of solutions tailored to meet your needs.

Fridge Repair

We provide expert repair services for household and commercial refrigerators. Whether your fridge is not cooling, has a leak, or is making unusual noises, our skilled technicians will diagnose and fix the issue efficiently.

Aircon Servicing

Regular aircon servicing is essential to maintain a healthy and comfortable environment. We offer thorough cleaning, filter replacements, and performance checks to ensure your air conditioning system operates at its best.

Regassing

Our regassing services cater to both refrigerators and air conditioning systems. We ensure optimal cooling performance by refilling the refrigerant gas to the correct levels, prolonging the lifespan of your appliances and improving energy efficiency.
